LTC2959 Sensor Notes

  • Notes on LTC2959 Sensor
  • Sensor has 50mV Sense Range
  • Formula: V=IR :: I(range)=V(range)/R
  • Resistor / Current Sense Range note: lower range = finer resolution current sensing
  • 0.050 Ohm sensor (there are 2 pcs) is the datasheet's reference value with 533nAh LSB
  • V(sensep) has 65V max limit, but as a practical matter, you are limited by your battery's ability to supply voltage.

LTC2959 Power-Up Sequence

When VDD rises above a threshold of approximately 1.45V, the LTC2959 generates an internal power-on reset (POR) signal that sets all registers to their default state. In the default state, the coulomb counter is active while the multi-purpose ADC operates in sleep mode. The accumulated charge register is set to mid-scale (80000000h) and all ADC channel outputs are set to 0000h. All threshold registers and the min-max tracking registers are set to their default values. The min/max tracking registers of the ADC will update upon completion of the first ADC conversion. The GPIO pin is configured as an analog input; the ALERT and Charge Complete functionalities are not enabled at startup.
